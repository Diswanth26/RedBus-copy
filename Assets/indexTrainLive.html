Check Live Train Running Status

In a world where everyone wants to stay updated with what is happening around them, staying on top of things is essential. From food delivery to online shopping, you would have definitely tracked the order. In the same way, you can also check the live train running status. Among the various innovations that make life easier, checking online train status is highly significant as it has made train travel more accessible and convenient. You can check live train status instantly on redRail, eliminating the need to stand in long queues to enquire about the train at railway stations. Several platforms offer this service to confirm the current train running status. Therefore, redRail, an authorized partner of IRCTC, assures that the information you receive about the train is accurate. Using the redRail app, you can decide when to reach the railway station.

What You Would Know about Live Train Status?

Checking live train status is highly beneficial for passengers while travelling. Besides providing flexibility to plan travel, it also helps you track the live location of your train, allowing you to reach the railway station at your convenience. By checking the train's running status, you can overcome the challenges of boarding a delayed train at the last minute. Besides, booking train tickets, tracking live train status are equally important to enhance your travelling experience. redRail is a reliable platform that provides accurate information about the train's running status. Check out the information when you check live train status.

  • Date and day of the journey.
  • Time of departure from the starting station.
  • Status and time of the train crossing from one station to another.
  • Total distance covered by the train.
  • Estimated time of arrival and departure from each station.
  • Distance between two intermediate stations.
  • Arrival and departure time taken from one intermediate station to another.
  • Platform number of every station the train stops at.
  • Information about the delay of the train.

Role of NTES in Checking Live Train Status

The National Train Enquiry System (NTES train Enquiry) is an advanced system introduced by the Indian railways. It helps passengers gather all kinds of information related to their train travel. The NTES is a relatively comprehensive system that provides passengers with real-time information about the current train running status through various interfaces.

Key Features of National Train Enquiry System

Some of the important features offered by NTES system are as follows:

  • Spot your train by entering the train number or name.
  • Enter the source to the destination name to stay informed about the live station. Also, check the trains running every 2, 4, and 8 hours.
  • Schedule your train with the name or number of the train, followed by the journey date.
  • Explore trains between stations by entering the destination to the source station, followed by the types of trains running between these stations.
  • Check PNR status.
  • Get regular updates about any delay, diversion, rescheduling, station timetable, parcel and heritage trains.

This easy-to-use platform's GPS tracking system allows you to spot your train from anywhere and at any time. Now, you can stay updated with your train's online train running status via the redRail mobile app.

How to Check IRCTC Train Running Status on redRail?

Using the redRail app or website will help you get accurate real-time updates about your train. Follow the step-by-step instructions to check the live train status on redRail.

Check Live Train Running Status using redRail App

  • Step 1: Download the redRail app on your mobile device.
  • Step 2: Click on the Live Status option.
  • Step 3: Enter the train number (the train number is mentioned on your ticket) along with the name of the station (the name of the station is optional)
  • Step 4: Click on the Spot my Train option to initiate the live tracking of your train.
  • Step 5: The live train running status is displayed on your screen.

Check Live Train Running Status using redRail Website

  • Step 1: Click on the redRail icon displayed at the top of the home page.
  • Step 2: Click on the Live Status option.
  • Step 3: Enter the train number (the train number is mentioned on your ticket) along with the name of the station (the name of the station is optional)
  • Step 4: Click on the Spot my Train option to display your train's live location on the screen.
